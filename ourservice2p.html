How to apply for loan?

1. Create an account
2. Fill in the form about your income, credit history, etc.
3. Click on ‘Profile’ beside ‘Our Service’
4. Go to “Digital Wallet” to choose either the card we provide or add your bank account for your loan
5. Key in the details for your loan
6. Wait for the money to go in your account and your all good to go
